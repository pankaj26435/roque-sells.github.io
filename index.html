About Julio

Tired of seeing people lose unimaginable amounts of money, time, and sanity on failed home purchase transactions, Julio launched Re-Connect to help people finally reach their dream of owning the right home - while making agents’ lives a whole lot easier. Today, he is infinitely passionate about helping people make smart financial decisions for their families. He believes in creative thinking and looking beyond the status quo to find inventive solutions.

Julio’s extensive 17 plus years of real estate, mortgage, underwriting, and loan origination experience, with background on both sides of the transaction, give him a unique competitive advantage. Most importantly, he brings what no other real estate agent can: empathy and the human touch. Julio wants to help his clients avoid disappointment by bringing their true and authentic affordability to the surface - proving there’s no need to “settle” for what you “think” you can afford.
